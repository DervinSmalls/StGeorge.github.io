Jeff is a Spokane based Actor, Director, Producer and Educator specializing in Shakespeare, Commedia, and Expressionism.

Jeff’s Professional career started in Portland where he preformed principally as a Shakespearian actor, traveling across Oregon and Washington performing outdoors in commedia inspired productions. Jeff is a versitile character actor playing a wide variety of roles. From The Nurse to Macbeth to Ariel Jeff has had the fortune to explore many corners of Shakespeares Works.

Jeff’s bucket list is to perform all of Shakespeares known works. Jeff’s passion for the Bard extends to sharing it with others. Seeking to bring Shakespeare training into the modern age Jeff produces a video series called You Too May Speak Good Words where he shares the fundementals of the specialized creature that is Shakespearian performance.
